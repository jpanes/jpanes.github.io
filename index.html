iScholar

Ateneo Online Scholarship Application and SA Management System (Web and Mobile)

The Online Scholarship Management System, is a system that helps student assistants to monitor their daily time records and applicants in their application process for scholarship grant. Primarily,the system is for the convenience of the applicants to apply online and to know the requirements needed to be submitted for such scholarship application as well as for the admission staff part to handle the information of the applicants and also some details about the scholarship. It also increases the effectiveness to promote the scholarships offered online because the information in the system is updated regularly regarding such scholarships like the announcements of dates when applicants can apply online as well as the renewal dates, and applicants who granted a scholarship. It also monitors if the submitted documents of the applicants are already verified. It also lessens the applicant’s consumed time going to the admissions office to be able to inquire about the scholarship offered and other details/informations e.g requirements, documents necessary for application, also for scholars to renew their scholarship for the reason that the system will provide a client side system that allows the applicants to view the details and information about it as well as for the scholars to renew their scholarships online by filling out the online renewal form.

Number, Bulls and Cows

A linux terminal game developed using C.

Number Bulls & Cows is an old code-breaking mind or paper and pencil game for two or more players. The program was created using the standard C libraries. It made use of sockets to pass values from one program to another in a connected network host.

How to Play: Both players, think of a four-digit number that they want the other player to guess. At each turn, each player types a four-digit number, and the program checks to see how close they are to the answer by giving: 1.) The number of Bulls – right digits in the right position. 2.) The number of Cows – right digits Both Players try to guess the answer in the fewest number of turns.
